
## Who They Are

16–27 year olds who want to build a startup but haven't started yet. students, recent grads, or people stuck in jobs they don't want. smart and hungry but paralyzed by not knowing where to begin. no technical co-founder, no funding, no network — yet

## What They Want

- A real path. not inspiration porn, but an actual starting point
- Proof that someone their age did it without connections or capital
- Frameworks for finding ideas, validating fast, and shipping something
- Honest takes on what building actually looks like day to day

## How to Talk to Them

- Peer energy — you're one step ahead, not looking down from above
- Challenge the excuses: "waiting for the perfect idea is procrastination"
- Show the messy middle, not just the wins
- Be specific: tools, timelines, costs, decisions
- Your age is an asset here — use it. they need to see themselves in you
