## Who They Are

Small business owners and operators, 5–50 employees. running a real business with real problems — not a side project. revenue range €200K–€10M. non-technical. time-poor. they've heard about AI but haven't touched it yet

## What They Want

- Clarity, not complexity. tell them what to do, not what's possible
- Proof it works for businesses like theirs, not for big tech
- Low-risk entry points — free tools, quick wins, no big commitments
- Someone to trust who isn't trying to sell them a €50K project

## How to Talk to Them

- Plain language. no jargon. if you use a term, explain it immediately
- Lead with the outcome, not the technology
- Reassure first, then educate — "this is simpler than you think"
- Use their world: operations, staff, clients, time, margin
- Never mention your age or frame yourself as a student/learner
