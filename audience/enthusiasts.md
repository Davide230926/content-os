
## Who They Are

Curious professionals and hobbyists interested in AI, marketing, distribution, and ops. not building anything right now but thinking about it. consume a lot of content. professionals in non-tech roles — marketers, ops people, generalists — who want to stay ahead

## What They Want

- Simplified breakdowns of what's actually happening in AI/tech
- Practical tools and tactics they can try without deep expertise
- "I can do this too" energy — achievable results, not intimidating ones
- Entry points: where to start, what to learn first, what matters vs. noise

## How to Talk to Them

- Conversational and encouraging, not challenging
- Explain every acronym and term — assume nothing
- Use analogies to things they already understand
- Keep it light: they're here to learn, not to be tested
- Celebrate small wins and low-effort starting points
